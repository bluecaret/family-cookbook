Fried Tofu in Broth (Agedashi Tofu)
Category: JapaneseTag: side, soup, tofu
½” cubes of firm tofu. Roll in cornstarch. Fry in ¼” oil 1-2 min per side. Serve over broth made with dashi, mirin, soy sauce, cornstarch slurry. Pour broth over tofu in bowl. Garnish w/ ginger, green onion, nori.
Grilled Salted Mackerel (Saba Shioyaki)
Author: Just One CookbookCategory: JapaneseTag: main, fish, mackerel
Baked in the oven, this Grilled Mackerel or Saba Shioyaki is the simplest fish recipe you can make on a busy weeknight. Serve steamed rice, miso soup, and a side salad to accompany this flavorful fish!
