noRecipe:
  - Soak red onion in water for a bit.
  - Boil broccoli.
  - Medium boil 2 eggs, quarter them.
  - Sauce - 3 tbsp mayo, 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp mirin, 1 tsp dashi powder, 1 tbsp sesame seeds.
  - Mix all together.
