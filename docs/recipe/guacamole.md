## Quick Recipe

Combine 2 cloves minced garlic, ¼ cup scallions or red onion, ¼ jalapeno minced. Add 2 avocados, mash with the back of a fork. Stir in 1 tbsp lime juice. Finish w/ cilantro and sea salt.
